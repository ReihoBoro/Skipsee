Safety Tips

Stay safe while chatting with strangers.

Protect Your Identity

Never share personal details

Do not reveal your full name, address, phone number, email, or social media handles to strangers.

Be careful with what is visible

Check your background before starting video. Ensure no personal documents or identifying items are visible.

Online Interactions

Trust your instincts

If you feel uncomfortable, skip immediately. You are never obligated to continue a conversation.

Beware of scams

Do not click on links sent by strangers. Be wary of anyone asking for money or claiming to be "in trouble."

Resources

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services.
