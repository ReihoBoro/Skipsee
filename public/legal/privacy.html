Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data on Skipsee.

Summary

We collect minimal data to operate the service (IP address, basic connection logs). We do NOT record video calls. We use local storage for your settings. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected: IP address, device type, browser information for connection and security purposes.

Consent Data: We store a record of your agreement to our Terms (IP, timestamp) to comply with legal requirements.

2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Connect you with other users (peer-to-peer).
  • Enforce bans for terms violations.
  • Analyze site traffic and performance.
3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use Local Storage to remember your preferences (e.g., dark mode, interests) and your consent status. We may use third-party cookies for analytics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may disclose information if required by law (e.g., subpoena) or to protect the safety of users.
