Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about using Skipsee.

General

Is Skipsee free?

Yes, Skipsee is completely free to use. We do not charge for basic random video chat features.

Do I need to register?

You can use the basic features as a guest, but logging in allows you to save preferences and access more features.

Technical Issues

My camera/microphone isn't working

Please check your browser permissions. Ensure you have allowed Skipsee to access your camera and microphone. Refresh the page after changing settings.

Why was I skipped?

Users can skip anyone for any reason. Don't take it personally! Just click "Next" to find someone new.

Safety

How do I report someone?

Use the "Report" button on the video screen if someone violates our guidelines. We take reports seriously.
