Community Guidelines

Help us keep Skipsee fun and safe for everyone.

The Golden Rule

Treat others as you would like to be treated. Be respectful, kind, and open-minded.

Do's
  • Be polite and respectful.
  • Skip if you are not interested, without being rude.
  • Report users who violate the rules.
  • Have fun and make new friends!
Don'ts
  • No Nudity or Sexual Acts: This is a strict ban.
  • No Harassment: Do not bully, insult, or threaten others.
  • No Hate Speech: Racism, sexism, and other forms of discrimination are not allowed.
  • No Advertising: Do not spam links or promote products.
Consequences

Violating these guidelines will result in an immediate ban from the platform. We may also report illegal content to relevant authorities.
