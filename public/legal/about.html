About Skipsee

Connecting the world, one random video call at a time.

Our Mission

Skipsee was created to bring people together from all corners of the globe. In an increasingly digital world, we believe in the power of face-to-face (digital) interaction to break down barriers and foster understanding.

How It Works

We use simple, direct peer-to-peer technology to connect you with a stranger. It's fast, free, and fun.

The Team

We are a small team of passionate developers working to build a safe and enjoyable platform.
